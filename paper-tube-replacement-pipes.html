Why Businesses Shift from Paper Cores

  • Paper cores can deform in repeated handling or moisture-prone conditions.
  • Roll structure stability becomes critical in storage and transport workflows.
  • Stronger core support helps reduce handling damage risk during dispatch.
  • Repeat-use scenarios often demand higher core durability than paper alternatives.

Application Segments

  • Cloth and fabric roll businesses.
  • Tarpaulin roll handling and distribution.
  • Packaging and winding operations.
  • Export packaging where roll stability is critical.
