Frequently Asked Questions

Which pipe is best for irrigation?

Both PVC and HDPE are used for irrigation. HDPE is often selected for flexible long routes, while PVC is preferred for structured and cost-focused layouts.

PVC vs HDPE pipes?

PVC pipes are rigid and economical for many applications. HDPE pipes are flexible and suitable for bending routes and high-impact requirements.

Cost of pipeline installation?

Installation cost depends on route length, trench depth, pipe type, and site conditions. Contact us for a quick estimate with practical sizing guidance.
