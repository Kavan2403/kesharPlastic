Choose PVC when…

  • The route is mostly straight and laid out in advance
  • You want rigid, structured water lines
  • Budget-conscious irrigation or contractor routing

Choose HDPE when…

  • The terrain is uneven or the route bends a lot
  • You need long runs with fewer joints
  • The handling environment is impact-prone
What decides the size

Four things we ask before recommending a diameter

01

Route length

Longer runs change both practical diameter and joint planning.

02

Flow need

How much water has to move points toward a minimum bore.

03

Terrain

Flat planned routes vs uneven bending routes affect PVC vs HDPE choice.

04

Application

Irrigation, industrial process or winding each carry different spec priorities.
